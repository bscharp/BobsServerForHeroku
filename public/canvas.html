One of the most importand HTML5 tools for rich applications is the canvas, a drawing surface. Compared with other HTML elements, the canvas is unique because it required Javascript. There's no way to draw shapes or paint pictures without it.

Choose the pen color and pen thickness from the 2 toolbars. Click on the canvas with the mouse button, move the mouse to draw a line and release the mouse button to end the line.
